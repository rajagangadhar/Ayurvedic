Ayurvedic Remedies for Cough

Natural Ayurvedic Remedies

Cough can be effectively managed with several Ayurvedic remedies. Here are some suggestions:

  • Honey and Ginger: Mixing honey with ginger juice helps soothe the throat and reduces coughing.
  • Tulsi Tea: Tulsi leaves, when brewed into tea, help in relieving cough and clearing respiratory pathways.
  • Turmeric Milk: Drinking warm turmeric milk helps to reduce cough and inflammation.
  • Steam Inhalation: Inhaling steam with a few drops of eucalyptus oil can help open airways and reduce cough.
