Ayurvedic Remedies for Fever

Natural Ayurvedic Remedies

Fever is a common health issue that can be treated effectively with Ayurvedic remedies. Here are some remedies:

  • Tulsi Leaves: Boil some tulsi leaves in water and drink it regularly to reduce fever.
  • Ginger Tea: A cup of ginger tea helps in sweating out toxins and lowering fever.
  • Turmeric Milk: Turmeric has anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce fever.
  • Coriander Tea: Coriander seeds boiled in water can help reduce fever naturally.
