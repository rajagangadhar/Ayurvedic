Ayurvedic Remedies for Headache

Natural Ayurvedic Remedies

Headaches can be caused by stress, dehydration, or other factors. Here are some Ayurvedic remedies to help relieve headaches:

  • Ginger Paste: Applying a paste of ginger powder and water on the forehead can help alleviate headaches.
  • Peppermint Oil: Massaging peppermint oil on the temples can reduce headache symptoms.
  • Basil Tea: Drinking basil tea can provide relief from tension headaches.
  • Cinnamon Paste: Make a paste of cinnamon and apply it on the forehead to soothe headache pain.
