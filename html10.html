Ayurvedic Remedies for High Blood Pressure

Natural Ayurvedic Remedies

High blood pressure can be managed with several Ayurvedic remedies. Here are some suggestions:

  • Garlic: Garlic helps in lowering blood pressure by relaxing the blood vessels.
  • Ashwagandha: This herb helps reduce stress, which is a key contributor to high blood pressure.
  • Amla: Rich in vitamin C, Amla helps in improving heart health and reducing blood pressure.
  • Yoga and Meditation: Regular practice of yoga and meditation can greatly reduce stress and help maintain normal blood pressure levels.
