Ayurvedic Remedies for Body Pains

Natural Ayurvedic Remedies

Body pains can be alleviated using various Ayurvedic remedies. Here are some effective ones:

  • Warm Oil Massage: Massaging with warm sesame oil or coconut oil can help relieve body pain and stiffness.
  • Turmeric Milk: Drinking warm milk with turmeric can reduce inflammation and provide relief from body aches.
  • Ginger Tea: Ginger tea has anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce body pain and discomfort.
  • Epsom Salt Bath: A bath with Epsom salt can help relax muscles and reduce body aches.
