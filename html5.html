Ayurvedic Remedies for Stomach Pain

Natural Ayurvedic Remedies

Stomach pain can be relieved using Ayurvedic remedies. Here are some remedies:

  • Ajwain (Carom Seeds): Chewing ajwain seeds or drinking ajwain water helps relieve indigestion and stomach pain.
  • Ginger Tea: Drinking ginger tea improves digestion and reduces stomach cramps.
  • Fennel Seeds: Consuming fennel seeds or fennel tea soothes stomach discomfort and bloating.
  • Aloe Vera Juice: Aloe vera has anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce stomach inflammation.
