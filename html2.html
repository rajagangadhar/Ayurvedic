Ayurvedic Remedies for Diabetes

Natural Ayurvedic Remedies

Diabetes can be effectively managed with several Ayurvedic remedies. Here are some suggestions:

  • Methi (Fenugreek): Methi seeds are known to control blood sugar levels.
  • Amla: Amla is rich in vitamin C and antioxidants, making it beneficial for managing diabetes.
  • Cinnamon: Cinnamon is known to improve insulin sensitivity and lower blood glucose levels.
  • Bitter Gourd: Consuming bitter gourd juice can help reduce blood sugar levels.
