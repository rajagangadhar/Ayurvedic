Ayurvedic Remedies for Knee Pain

Natural Ayurvedic Remedies

Knee pain can be managed effectively with Ayurvedic remedies. Here are some remedies:

  • Turmeric and Ginger Paste: Apply a paste of turmeric and ginger on the knees to reduce pain and inflammation.
  • Mustard Oil Massage: Regular massage with mustard oil can help alleviate knee pain.
  • Epsom Salt Soak: Soaking the knees in Epsom salt water can help relieve pain and reduce inflammation.
  • Fenugreek Seeds: Consuming fenugreek seeds or applying a paste of fenugreek seeds can help with knee pain.
